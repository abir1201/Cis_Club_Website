About the CIS Club

The CIS Club of Daffodil International University is a student-driven, tech-focused community dedicated to learning, innovation, and professional growth. Our mission is to inspire students of the Computer & Information Systems (CIS) program to explore cutting-edge technology, develop real-world skills, and prepare for future careers in the IT industry.

We organize workshops, seminars, coding sessions, project showcases, and industry-expert talks to help students enhance their technical and soft skills. The club also encourages collaboration, leadership, networking, and creativity—allowing members to grow both academically and professionally.

With a vision to build a strong and vibrant tech community, the CIS Club strives to empower students, support innovative ideas, and create opportunities that shape tomorrow's technology leaders.

Learn • Collaborate • Innovate

Curriculum

The CIS Club Curriculum is designed to help students build strong technical foundations while gaining practical, industry-relevant experience. Our learning path is structured into multiple skill areas, ensuring members can grow step-by-step based on their interests and career goals.

1. Programming Fundamentals

  • C, C++, Java, Python Basics
  • Problem Solving & Logic Building
  • Data Structures & Algorithms (Intro)

2. Web & App Development

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Front-end Frameworks (React / Vue – optional)
  • Backend Basics (PHP, Node.js)
  • Mobile App Fundamentals

3. Database & Information Systems

  • SQL & NoSQL Databases
  • Data Modeling
  • Basics of Management Information Systems (MIS)

4. Cybersecurity Basics

  • Networking Fundamentals
  • Ethical Hacking Introduction
  • Cyber Hygiene & Safety Practices

5. Cloud & DevOps (Introductory)

  • Cloud Computing Concepts
  • Git & Version Control
  • Continuous Integration Basics

6. Soft Skills & Professional Development

  • Presentation Skills
  • Team Collaboration
  • Industry Career Guidance Sessions
